Welcome to the Expense Tracker

Manage your expenses effortlessly and track your financial goals with ease. Our Expense Tracking Web App provides you with all the tools you need to take control of your finances.

What is Expense Tracking?

Expense tracking is the process of recording all the money you spend, helping you understand where your money goes and how to budget effectively. With our app, you can:

  • Track your daily expenses.
  • Set monthly budget limits.
  • Visualize spending patterns with easy-to-read charts.
  • Get notifications for over-budget categories.

Key Features

Our Expense Tracker comes with several powerful features to help you track your finances more efficiently:

  • Easy-to-use interface with a user-friendly dashboard.
  • Category-based expense tracking (e.g., food, transport, entertainment).
  • Ability to add recurring expenses for easy tracking.
  • Generate monthly or yearly reports.
  • Multi-device sync to track expenses on any platform.

How to Get Started

To get started, simply sign up for a free account and add your first expense. Follow these steps:

  1. Sign up and log in to your account.
  2. Go to the "Add Expense" section and input your first expense.
  3. Choose a category (e.g., Groceries, Entertainment, Bills).
  4. Set a budget limit for each category, if desired.
  5. Monitor your expenses and adjust your budget as necessary!
